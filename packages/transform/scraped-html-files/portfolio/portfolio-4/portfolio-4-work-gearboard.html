The Ask

Ruggable is a household name in the world of interior furnishings. The idea behind Wallnut was to utilize their knowledge of home goods market and expertise as a leading online retailer to offer quality artworks to a similarly stylish and sophisticated audience at accessible prices.

The Design

Walnut's design strategy incorporates the parent company's established home goods aesthetic and market expertise to create artwork that enhances modern interiors while ensuring it remains stylish and budget-friendly.

Light up an industry by lighting up the room.

the development

Walnut's development process combines the parent company’s extensive knowledge of home furnishings with cutting-edge art production techniques to produce a refined and diverse collection of high-quality, accessible artwork.

the takeaway

Walnut effectively leverages the parent company’s reputation to introduce a sophisticated range of affordable artwork, successfully bridging the gap between home decor and art while appealing to a tastefully discerning audience.
