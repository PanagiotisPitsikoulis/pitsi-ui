Home, But Smarter

Ask less. Know more

Quick answers to the things you’re wondering—so you can get back to living smarter.

What is Quora and what does it do?

Quora is a smart home device brand that connects and controls your home appliances, lighting, security, and more through a single app or voice commands. It brings convenience, automation, and energy efficiency to your everyday life.

How do I set up my Quora device?

Simply plug in your device, download the Quora Smart app from the App Store or Google Play, and follow the step-by-step setup guide. Most devices can be connected to your Wi-Fi and configured in under 5 minutes.

Is Quora compatible with Alexa, Google Assistant, or Siri?

Yes! Quora smart devices are compatible with Amazon Alexa, Google Assistant, and Apple Siri Shortcuts, allowing you to control your home with simple voice commands.

Can I control my Quora devices when I'm not at home?

Absolutely. As long as your devices are connected to Wi-Fi and you have internet access on your phone, you can control, schedule, and monitor them from anywhere using the Quora app.

Is my data safe with Quora?

Yes. We take your privacy seriously. Quora uses end-to-end encryption and follows industry-standard data protection practices to keep your personal information secure.

Do Quora devices require a hub?

Most Quora devices are hub-free and connect directly to your Wi-Fi network. However, certain automation features or legacy products may require the Quora Smart Hub for optimal performance.

Can I set up automation or routines with Quora devices?

Yes. You can create custom schedules, routines, and triggers in the Quora Smart app—for example, turning on lights at sunset or activating a security mode when you leave home.

What should I do if my device isn’t responding?

If your Quora device isn’t responding, start by making sure it’s connected to power and your Wi-Fi network.
